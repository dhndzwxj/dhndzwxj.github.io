f(\textbf{x})=f(x_1,x_2,\cdots,x_n) $$ 由$f(\textbf{x})$的一阶偏导数构成的行向量 $$ f_x(\textbf{x}) = \left(\frac{\partial f(\textbf{x})}{\partial x_1},\frac{\partial f(\textbf{x})}{\partial x_2},\cdots,\frac{\partial f(\textbf{x})}{\partial x_n}\right) $$ 称为$f(\textbf{x})$的**梯度**。 #### 3.3.1 链式法则和方向导数 考虑函数$f(\textbf{x})=f(x_1,x_2,\cdots,x_n)$，假设函数$f(\textbf{x})$的偏导数 $$ \frac{\partial f(\textbf{x})}{\partial x_1},\frac{\partial f(\textbf{x})}{\partial x_2},\cdots,\frac{\partial f(\textbf{x})}{\partial x_n} $$ 存在且连续，并且单变量$\lambda\in E^1$的函数$\varphi_j(\lambda)$具有一阶连续导数，将 $$ x_1=\varphi_1(\lambda),x_2=\varphi_2(\lambda),\cdots,x_n=\varphi_n(\lambda) $$ 代入到$f(x_1,x_2,\cdots,x_n)$中，得到关于单变量$\lambda$的函数，得到一个复合函数。 $$ f\left(\varphi_1(\lambda),\varphi_2(\lambda),\cdots,\varphi_n(\lambda)\right) $$ 下面是所谓的“链式法则”的表达式： $$ \begin{aligned} \frac{\text{d}f\left(\varphi_1(\lambda),\varphi_2(\lambda),\cdots,\varphi_n(\lambda)\right)}{\text{d}\lambda} &= \left.\sum_{j=1}^n\frac{\partial f(\textbf{x})}{\partial x_j}\cdot\varphi'_j(\lambda)\right\lvert_{\textbf{x}=(\varphi_1(\lambda),\varphi_2(\lambda),\cdots,\varphi_n(\lambda))^T}\notag\\ &= \sum_{j=1}^n\frac{\partial f\left( \varphi_1(\lambda),\varphi_2(\lambda),\cdots,\varphi_n(\lambda) \right)}{\partial x_j}\cdot\varphi'_j(\lambda) \notag\\ &= f_x(\varphi_1(\lambda),\varphi_2(\lambda),\cdots,\varphi_n(\lambda))\cdot(\varphi'_1(\lambda),\phi'_2(\lambda),\cdots,\phi'_n(\lambda))^T \end{aligned} $$ --- 例3.2 对于无约束极值问题 $$
